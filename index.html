About Storied

Storied is a modern adaption of the original fairy tale of The Little Mermaid by Hans Christian Andersen. The story is rewritten as electronic literature and in poetry form the story is re-told through the Little mermaid her self. If you are unfamilar with The Little Mermaid, the story follows the journey of a young mermaid in her search for a human soul and what she is willing to sacrifice for it.

How to read

Simply press a word that is emphasized with a bold font to load a new poem. The words you press gets registered in the coockies and can have an impact on the poems you get.
